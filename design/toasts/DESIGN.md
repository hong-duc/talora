# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our design system.

## Theme Overview

The current theme is set to `dark` mode, emphasizing accessibility and a reduced strain on the eyes in low-light environments.

## Color Palette

Our brand utilizes a rich and distinct color palette to evoke specific emotions and ensure clear visual hierarchy.

-   **Primary Color:** `#7311d4` - A vibrant purple that serves as our brand's most distinctive chromatic color, ideal for calls to action and key interactive elements.
-   **Secondary Color:** `#8a66ba` - A supporting lavender hue for less prominent UI elements, chips, and secondary actions.
-   **Tertiary Color:** `#883f00` - A warm, earthy brown serving as an additional accent for highlights, badges, or decorative elements.
-   **Neutral Color:** `#7c7481` - A balanced neutral tone used for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements, providing a solid foundation for the chromatic palette.

## Typography

The typography strategy focuses on readability and brand consistency.

-   **Headline Font:** `newsreader` - Chosen for its strong character and impact in headlines.
-   **Body Font:** `newsreader` - Selected for legibility and consistency in longer text passages.
-   **Label Font:** `newsreader` - Used for clear and concise labels across the UI.

## Shape and Form

The visual language of our components is defined by their roundedness and spacing.

-   **Roundedness:** A moderate roundedness (`2`) is applied to UI elements, offering a balanced and friendly aesthetic without being overly soft or sharp.
-   **Spacing:** A `compact` spacing (`2`) is maintained throughout the UI, striking a balance between information density and visual comfort.
